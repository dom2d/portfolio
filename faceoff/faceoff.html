FACEOFF

Find the henchmen and villain aliens based on clues about their appearance!

How to Play

Goal

Find 2 henchmen and 1 villain using the clues provided.

Selection

Click faces to select them. The first two selections highlight in gold (henchmen), the third selection highlights in red (villain).

A/B/C Checkboxes

Use these to mark faces you've ruled out:

  • A - Ruled out by Suspect Alpha clues
  • B - Ruled out by Suspect Beta clues
  • C - Ruled out by Villain clues

Understanding Clues

Direct neighbors = faces directly adjacent (up/down/left/right)

Diagonal neighbors = faces touching diagonally

Same row/column = other faces in the same horizontal row or vertical column

The Villain

The villain sits at the intersection of the two henchmen - where one henchman's column meets the other's row (or vice versa). Once you find the henchmen, only 2 faces will be at this intersection. The final villain clue tells you which one it is.
