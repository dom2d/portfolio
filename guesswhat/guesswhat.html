How to Play

Goal: Find the ONE person who fits ALL the clues using the fewest clues possible.

Gameplay:

  1. Click clue cards to reveal them one at a time
  2. Eliminate people who match each clue by clicking their card
  3. Click again to undo if you make a mistake
  4. When only one person remains, click "Check Answer"

Scoring:
Fewer clues used = better score! Try to solve it with as few reveals as possible.

Tip: Process clues carefully. Each clue eliminates people who match that description, so the remaining person is the answer.
